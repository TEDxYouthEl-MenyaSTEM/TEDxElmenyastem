Omar Al-Gabaly is a 27-year-old who teaches communications engineering and lives in the Giza governorate.

Omar Gabaly seemed during high school to think of a new sport called Street Workout and decided to train this sport at home beside his studies.

Omar Al-Gabaly became the most important player for the Street Workout in Egypt then he went abroad and challenged competitors from all over the world and achieved great achievements and impressed all the young Egyptians got a world-class position.

You can imagine the presence of Omar Gabaly, who founded this sport in his country and is now creating tournaments for young Egyptians, which we often see daily in Facebook and Instagram videos.

Street Workout is the new definition of gymnastics (bodyweight exercises). It is particularly popular in New York City, Miami, Los Angeles, Philadelphia, and Chicago.

Omar followed what he adores from the depths of his heart, becoming a professional, take this as an example that achieving success does not have a specific age. Look where your heart will take you in life. Stay tuned for more

Astronomy

Essam Goda

Space scout

Essam Goda

+

"I loved the sky since childhood, so I soared in it with my soul and mind drawing from its rich sea of sciences." - Essam Goda - Space Scout

Essam Godastudied Science and Engineering, where he obtained a Bachelor of Engineering and then a Diploma in Space dynamics from the Faculty of Science, Cairo University.

He began his astronomical activity with a membership in the Astronomical Society of Mostafa Mahmoud. Then, to further develop the dissemination of astronomy in the community, he co-founded the Egyptian Society for Astronomy and became the Chairman of its Board of Directors. Furthermore, he also became a member of the Arab Union for Astronomy and Space Sciences.

He communicates sciences by holding lectures in space and astronomy for the public. He also participated in preparing and presenting the first Egyptian documentary film on astronomy, produced by ON-E, and was hosted on many talk shows.

Essam also offers seminars and training courses in space sciences and astronomy in numerous cultural centers, public libraries, universities, and online platforms.

His motto was and will always be "Sky is my home" ✨🌌
